Cuyler Warnock


I am an assistant professor of mathematics at Wesleyan College, the first college in the world chartered to grant degrees to women. Currently, I am the only mathematics professor at the college.


I obtained my Ph.D. in mathematics at the University of South Carolina. My advisor was Dr. Matthew Boylan and my research is in the area of modular forms. In particular, I am interested in how the Hecke operator permutes certain subspaces.


I received a Bachelor's degree in mathematics from Georgia College & State University with a minor in computer science.
